About Black hole

A black hole is a region of spacetime exhibiting such strong gravitational effects that nothing not even particles and electromagnetic radiation such as light can escape from inside it.

Black hole interesting facts

  1. Black holes form from the remnants of a large star that dies in a supernova explosion.
  2. Scientists used event horizon telescope to take a photo of M87 black hole
  3. It took eight telescopes across the planet a week of observations to produce the black hole image
